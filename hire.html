Hire Me

About

Hi, I am Hamel Husain. As a machine learning engineer with over 20 years of experience, I have worked with innovative companies such as Airbnb and GitHub and led and contributed to numerous popular open-source machine-learning tools. I am currently an independent consultant helping companies operationalize Large Language Models (LLMs) to accelerate their AI product journey. You can read more about my experience here.

You should consider hiring me if …

  • You don’t know how to improve your LLM products replicably.
  • Your LLMs need to be cheaper or faster.
  • You are overwhelmed by tools & frameworks.
  • Feel lost on what to do next with your LLM products.
  • Need outside expertise to evaluate your needs and vet potential talent.

What I can do for you.

  • Build domain-specific evaluation systems: Measure the performance and reliability of your LLMs with custom evaluation systems tailored to your domain.
  • Fine-tune models: Optimize your LLMs for efficiency and accuracy to enhance the performance of your products.
  • Create development tools and infrastructure: Streamline your AI development process with custom-built tools and infrastructure, enabling rapid iteration on LLMs.
  • Product strategy: I can guide you on what to build, how to build it, and what to prioritize. I can also assist with hiring by evaluating your business’ needs and vetting potential talent.
  • Content and writing: Make your AI solutions understandable to users and stakeholders.

If you have experienced any of these problems and are looking for help solving them, send me a brief email at hamel@parlance-labs.com about your project’s needs.

Services

I offer two tiers of services to support your goals.

Tier 1: Strategy

I will advise you on the following topics:

  • LLM performance issues: (cost, quality, speed)
  • Product Strategy
  • Custom Evaluation Systems
  • RAG, Fine-Tuning, and Prompt Engineering

I’ll give you a detailed plan and help you stay on track via regular meetings with your team. This is designed to help you save enormous amounts of time and money by avoiding common pitfalls and selecting the right tools and techniques.

Tier 2: Comprehensive

Everything in Tier 1, plus:

  • Implementation: I’ll write production-ready code and/or prototypes to accelerate your AI product development.
  • Hands-On Model Optimization: I’ll fine-tune, prompt engineer and debug models to improve performance and efficiency.
  • Content and Writing: Produce written documents and blogs to communicate best practices, methodologies, and practical case studies to your stakeholders
  • Team Growth: Work with 2-4 people individually on your team to rapidly upskill them on AI.
  • Extended Consultation and Research: In-depth consultations on advanced AI topics, including state-of-the-art techniques and emerging trends.

I only work with one Tier 2 client at a time to ensure quality of service.

I am available on a retainer basis for short-term and long-term engagements. Contact me at hamel@parlance-labs.com for details on rates and payment or to start an engagement.
